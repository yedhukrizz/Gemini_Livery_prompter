Workflow Guide

Follow these steps to apply a reference livery style to your vehicle using AI (Google Gemini).

1

Upload Reference Image

Prepare the image of the livery design or color scheme you want to copy. Save this image to your device.

2

Upload Vehicle Image

Prepare the image of the target car (the vehicle model you entered in the sidebar). Save this image too.

3

Configure & Copy

Use the sidebar controls to configure your style. Click Generate JSON, then click the Copy button.

4

Paste into Gemini

Open Google Gemini. Attach both your Reference Image and Vehicle Image. Then paste the copied JSON text into the chat box.

5

See the Magic Happen

Send the prompt. Gemini will analyze the livery from the reference and apply it to your vehicle based on your configuration.
